PrimBooks Product Requirements Document (PRD)- Updated Version
Prepared by: Oluwadamilola Oluwaniyi (Product Manager)
1. Product Overview
PrimBooks is an all-in-one real time cloud based financial and business management software designed to help small and medium sized businesses manage their finances and business operations from a single integrated platform.
Many businesses currently rely on spreadsheets, manual bookkeeping, notebooks, and disconnected tools to run different parts of their operations such as accounting, customer management, inventory, payroll, and reporting. As these businesses grow, these systems become difficult to manage and often result in poor financial visibility, weak internal control, inefficient workflows, and limited accountability.
PrimBooks solves this problem by providing a unified platform that connects finance, operations, compliance, reporting, and business control in one system.
Through PrimBooks, businesses can manage:
financial accounting
records and transaction flow
customer and vendor relationships
inventory and production
HR and payroll
asset management
tax compliance
bank reconciliation
reporting and audit visibility
Because PrimBooks is cloud based, users can access data in real time from anywhere. The platform is designed to be simple enough for non-accountants and structured enough for accountants, auditors, and growing businesses that need stronger control.
PrimBooks functions as a central financial and business operating system for SMEs.

4. Target Users
Small Business Owners: Need a simple way to manage finances, operations, and reporting.
Finance Teams and Accountants: Need structured records, reconciliation tools, and financial reporting.
Internal Auditors: Need traceable financial activity and visibility into who did what.
HR and Operations Managers: Need employee records, payroll support, attendance visibility, and structured workflows.
Freelancers and Professionals: Need invoicing, expense tracking, and simple financial summaries.
Growing SMEs: Need more than bookkeeping. They need control, accountability, reconciliation, asset tracking, and business visibility.
5. Core Problems PrimBooks Solves
1. Poor Financial Visibility: Many SMEs cannot clearly see their true revenue, expenses, profit position, and cash movement because their records are fragmented.
2. Bank Reconciliation Challenges: Many businesses struggle with bank reconciliation because systems become difficult to use or unstable as transaction volumes increase. PrimBooks addresses this with a reliable reconciliation workflow and strong transaction matching capability. This is one of the major selling points of the product.
3. Lack of Financial Accountability: As more users begin handling financial records, businesses need to know who created, changed, or deleted data. PrimBooks solves this through a built-in audit trail.
4. Weak Asset Tracking: Many SMEs cannot properly track assets, depreciation, or asset-related transactions. PrimBooks addresses this through structured asset management.
5. Disconnected Customer and Sales Workflows: Customer records, orders, quotations, invoices, and credit notes are often scattered or manually managed. PrimBooks centralizes these through CRM.
6. Operational Fragmentation: Inventory, purchases, and production often operate outside the finance system. PrimBooks connects them so operational activity supports financial accuracy.
7. Growing HR and Payroll Complexity: As businesses grow, they require better employee management, salary process visibility, and payroll-related control. PrimBooks provides a structured HR and Payroll module.
6. Core Product Modules
6.1 Dashboard
The dashboard is the entry point into PrimBooks and provides a real time summary of business performance.
Current dashboard capabilities include:
light mode, dark mode, and system mode
KPI cards for sales, orders, expenses, customers, and vendors
business insight widgets
overview charts
Purpose: To help users quickly understand the state of the business at a glance.

The Record module supports structured financial and operational entries that feed into the broader system.
Purpose: To centralize transaction-related records and ensure that business events are properly captured inside PrimBooks. This module is one of the foundational working areas in the product.
